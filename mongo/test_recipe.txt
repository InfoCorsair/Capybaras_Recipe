Recipe:  
  Spaghetti & Meatballs
Cook Time
  1 hour 5 minutes 
Serving Size
  8 servings
Ingredients
  3 diced slices of white bread with crust removed
  ⅔ cup cold water
  1 lb lean ground beef (7-15%)
  1lb Sweet Ground italian sausage (casings removed)
  ¼ cup grated parmesan cheese
  8 minced garlic gloves
  1 tsp sea salt
  ½ tsp black pepper
  1 large egg
  ¾ cup all-purpose flour
  3 tbsp light olive oil 
  1 medium yellow onion 
  56 oz crushed tomatoes 
  2 tbsp finely minced basil
  1 lb spaghetti
  2 bay leaves 
Appliances
  Stovetop
Dietary
  Includes Meat
  Meal Type 
  
  
Steps
  1. Combine bread pieces with ⅔ cup water and set aside for 5 minutes then mash and with a fork.
  2. In a large mixing bowl add: 1 lb ground beef, 1 lb sausage, ¼ cup parmesan, 4 minced garlic cloves, 1 tsp salt, ½ tsp black pepper, 1 egg and mashed bread crumbs.
  3. Mix until well combined.
  4. Form 1 ½’’ meat balls.
  5. Dredge/roll meatballs in flour, dust off excess.
  6. Head a deep,large skillet over medium heat with 3 tbsp oil.
  7. Add meatballs in batches without crowding the pan.
  8. Saute until browned on all sides, about 6 minutes in total.
  9. Remove meatballs and set aside.
 10. In the same skillet over medium heat, add more if needed and saute 1 cup chopped onion until soft and golden.
 11. Add 4 cloves of minced garlic and stir for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
 12. Add crushed tomatoes and bay leaves. Bring to a light boil.
 13. Add meatballs back into the pan with tomato sauce. Partially cover with a lid.
 14. Let it simmer for 30 minutes, occasionally turning the meatballs. 
 15. While waiting on the sauce, cook the pasta according to the package. Drain and return to the pot.
 16. Pour sauce and meatballs over spaghetti and toss gently to combine.
 17. Add the chopped basil leaves on top.
