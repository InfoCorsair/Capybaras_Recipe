Recipe
  Brownies 
Cook Time
  45 Minutes 
Serving Size
  16 servings
Ingredients
  1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  ¾ all purpose flour
  ⅔ cocoa powder
  ½ cup powdered sugar
  ½ cup dark chocolate chips
  ¾ tsp sea salt
  2 large eggs 
  ½ cup canola oil
  2 tbsp water
  ½ tps vanilla
  Parchment Paper
  Cooking Spray
Appliances
  Oven 
  8x8 Baking Dish
Dietary
  Vegetarian
Meal Type 
  Dessert

Steps
  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F.
  2. Lightly spray an 8x8 baking dish with cooking spray and line with parchment paper.
  3. Spray the parchment paper.
  4. In a medium bowl, combine sugar, flour, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, chocolate chips, and seasalt.
  5. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, oil, water, and vanilla. 
  6. Sprinkle the dry mix over the wet mix and stir until combined.
  7. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
  8. When the oven is ready, put the pan into the oven.
  9. Cook for 40 to 48 minutes, or until the toothpick comes out with only a few crumbs attached. 
 10. Let the brownies cool completely before you slice.
