Recipe
  Fried Egg
Cook Time
  5 Minutes
Serving Size
  1 servings 
Ingredients:
  1 TBSP. butter
  1 Large egg
  Pinch of kosher salt 
  Pinch of black pepper
Appliances
  Stove Top
  Frying Pan
Dietary
  Vegetarian
Meal Type 
  Breakfast

Steps
  1. In a small non-stick pan over medium heat, melt butter.
  2. Crack egg into pan after butter is fully melted.
  3. Cook for 3 minutes, or until white is set.
  4. Flip and cook the other side for 30 seconds until white is set.
  5. Remove the pan and season with salt and pepper.
