Recipe
  Ribs
Cook Time
  2 Hours 15 minutes
Serving Size
  6 servings
Ingredients
  6 pounds baby back pork ribs
  3 tsp garlic powder
  1 ½ tsp onion powder
  3 tsp paprika
  4 ½  tsp salt
  1 ½ tsp black pepper
  ¾ tsp cumin
  1 ½ tsp cayenne powder or chili powder
  6 tbsp olive oil
  3 cups bbq sauce
  4 ½ tbsp minced garlic
  1 ½ tbsp worcestershire sauce 
  1 ½ tbsp cayenne pepper 
Appliances
  Oven
Dietary
  Includes Meat
Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Peel off tough membrane that covers underside/bony side of rubs
  3. Place baking sheet or tray lined with foil
  4. Combine together garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, pepper, 1 ½ tsp cayenne.
  5. Sprinkle seasoning over ribs and drizzle with 3 tbsp oil 
  6. Bake for two hours.
  7. With 5 minutes left on the rubs, mix together bbq sauce, minced garlic 3 tbsp olive oil, worcestershire sauce, 1 ½ cayenne pepper.
  8. Remove ribs from the oven when done.
  9. Remove foil and spread the tops of the ribs with the mixture.
 10. Increase the temperature of the oven to 460°F.
 11. Return ribs uncovered back into the oven.
 12. Bake for an additional 10 minutes. 
 13. When the ten minutes are up, change the oven to broil(or grill) to lightly char the edges for 3 minutes. 
 14. Let rest for 10 minutes.
