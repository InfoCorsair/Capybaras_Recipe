Recipe
 Vegetable Stir Fry
Cook Time:
  15 Minutes
Serving Size
  6 servings 
Ingredients
  1 tbsp olive oil
  1 sliced red bell pepper
  1 sliced yellow bell pepper
  1 cup sugar snap peas
  1 cup sliced carrots
  1 cup sliced mushrooms
  2 cups broccoli
  1 cup baby corn
  ½ cup water chestnuts
  ¼ cup soy sauce
  3 minced garlic cloves
  3 tbsp brown sugar
  1 tsp sesame oil 
  ½ chicken broth
  1 tbsp cornstarch
  Optional - Chopped green onions and sesame seeds for garnish 
Appliances
  Stovetop
  Frying Pan
Dietary
  Vegetarian
  Vegan
Meal Type 
  Lunch

Steps
  1. Add 1 tbsp olive oil over medium-high heat in a large skillet.
  2. Add bell peppers, peas, carrots, mushrooms, broccoli, baby corn, and water chestnuts into the skillet.
  3. Saute vegetables for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are almost tender. 
  4. While vegetables are cooking, whisk together soy sauce, garlic, brown sugar, sesame oil, chicken broth, and cornstarch in a bowl. 
  5. Pour the contents of the bowl over the veggies.
  6. Cook until the sauce has thickened. 
  7. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds if desired.
